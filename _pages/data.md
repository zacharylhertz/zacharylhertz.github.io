I have compiled and created several original datasets on state-level election results. My current project involves a dataset examining minority turnout in local California elections. I plan to make these datasets available here; for now, please [send me an email](mailto:zach.l.hertz@gmail.com) for more information and I'd be happy to share!

I am also experienced using `ggplot2` to create data visualizations from complex datasets. I have uploaded [a selection of my work](https://zacharylhertz.github.io/data-viz/) for your perusal.
