Welcome to my website! My name is Zachary Lorico Hertz and I am a political science researcher seeking opportunities in social science research and data analysis. I graduated from [Tufts University](https://www.tufts.edu/) in 2019 with a Bachelor of Arts in Political Science focusing on American Politics. In my other coursework, I studied the intersection of philosophy and public policy through the program in Ethics, Law and Society.  

I am broadly interested in the mechanisms through which people form and utilize political power in the United States, as well as the historical racial disparities in electoral participation. Generally, I aim to examine mass political behavior through a quantitative analytical lens. In current ongoing research, I am combining voter data and census data to analyze city council turnout on the district level.

Most recently, I worked as Student Outreach Coordinator at the [Jonathan M. Tisch College of Civic Life](https://tischcollege.tufts.edu/) at Tufts University, where I coordinated [JumboVote](https://tischcollege.tufts.edu/civic-practice/jumbovote), the university-wide voter registration and GOTV initiative. I also assisted with publishing reports at the [Institute for Democracy & Higher Education](https://idhe.tufts.edu/). Previously, I served as [Tufts FOCUS](https://students.tufts.edu/orientation/pre-orientation/focus-first-year-orientation-community-service) coordinator and a managing editor of [the Tufts Daily](https://tuftsdaily.com/author/zacharyhertz/).

While much of my time is devoted to reading, writing, or coding, I enjoy hiking, [quiz bowl](https://www.naqt.com/about/quiz-bowl.html), and both performing and producing music in my spare time. [Send me an email](mailto:zach.l.hertz@gmail.com) and let's talk more!
