Welcome to my website! My name is Zachary Lorico Hertz and I am a political science researcher with experience in social science research, political consulting, and data analysis. Currently, I am a Ph.D. student studying political science at UC Berkeley. I received a Master of Arts with a concentration in [Quantitative Methods and Social Analysis](https://voices.uchicago.edu/qrmeth/mapss-qmsa/) from the University of Chicago in 2022, and previously graduated from Tufts University in 2019 with a Bachelor of Arts in Political Science focusing on American Politics. In my other coursework, I studied the intersection of philosophy and public policy through the program in Ethics, Law and Society. [Click here](/cv/) to view my CV.

I am broadly interested in the mechanisms through which people form and utilize political power in the United States, as well as the historical racial disparities in electoral participation. I also study public opinion and survey methodology. Generally, I aim to examine mass political behavior through a quantitative analytical lens. In current ongoing research, I am combining voter data and census data to analyze city council turnout on the district level.

In addition to my academic research, I have done independent political consulting for [Groundwork Project](https://www.groundworkproject.com/about-us/), [Data for Progress](https://www.dataforprogress.org/), and independent clients.Most recently, I worked as a Polling Analyst at [Data for Progress](https://www.dataforprogress.org/), where I designed and fielded surveys for clients, primarily as part of the electoral team, and worked on methodological project seeking to understand candidate quality and survey accuracy. Previously, I was the Chapter Organizing Fellow at [Scholars Strategy Network](https://scholars.org/), where I helped build communities of scholars nationally and connect researchers with legislators and journalists to inform local policy. I also worked as Student Outreach Coordinator at the [Jonathan M. Tisch College of Civic Life](https://tischcollege.tufts.edu/) at Tufts University, where I coordinated [JumboVote](https://tischcollege.tufts.edu/civic-practice/jumbovote), the university-wide voter registration and GOTV initiative. At Tisch, I also assisted with publishing reports at the [Institute for Democracy & Higher Education](https://idhe.tufts.edu/).

While much of my time is devoted to reading, writing, or coding, I enjoy hiking, [quiz bowl](https://www.naqt.com/about/quiz-bowl.html), and both performing and producing music in my spare time. [Send me an email](mailto:zachary_hertz@berkeley.edu) and let's talk more!
